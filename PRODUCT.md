# AZHAR — Shariah Finance Compliance & Management Platform

## Brand & Product Identity
- **Name**: AZHAR
- **Tagline**: Shariah-Compliant Financial Intelligence for the Modern Islamic Economy
- **Audience**: Islamic banks, financial institutions, Shariah compliance officers, Muslim businesses, sovereign treasury operators
- **Tone**: Authoritative, trustworthy, precise, ethically grounded — professional fintech with deep Islamic finance credibility
- **Anti-references**: No flashy crypto aesthetics, no gambling/speculative imagery, nothing that evokes Riba or interest-based systems

## Strategic Principles
1. Every feature must reflect Shariah compliance as a first-class concern, not an afterthought
2. Data clarity above decoration — compliance officers need dense, readable dashboards
3. Trust through transparency — all audit trails, fatwa references, and Nisab thresholds must be visible
4. The platform is a tool for human Shariah boards, not a replacement for them

## MVP Feature Set

### 1. Transaction Compliance Screening
- Real-time screening of financial transactions against a Haram category database
- Flagged categories: Alcohol, Gambling/Maysir, Conventional Weapons, Pork/Non-Halal Food, Tobacco, Adult Entertainment, Conventional Interest (Riba)
- Status labels: Halal (green), Haram (red), Doubtful/Mashbooh (amber)
- Drill-down transaction detail with reason codes and fatwa references

### 2. Zakat Calculator & Nisab Tracker
- Calculates Zakat obligation based on asset types: Gold, Silver, Cash, Trade Goods, Livestock, Agricultural Produce, Business Assets
- Live Nisab threshold tracking (gold: 85g, silver: 595g) with current market price feed
- Zakat rate: 2.5% on Nisab-qualifying wealth held for one Hawl (lunar year)
- Distribution ledger: records where Zakat is allocated (Asnaf categories)

### 3. Halal Investment Screener
- Screen stocks, funds, and instruments against Shariah criteria
- Criteria: Revenue from Haram activities <5%, Debt-to-assets ratio <33%, Interest income <33%, Prohibited business sectors
- Compliance rating: Fully Compliant / Conditionally Compliant / Non-Compliant
- Purification amount calculator for conditionally compliant instruments

### 4. Shariah Audit Dashboard (Bank Operators)
- Aggregate view of all flagged transactions by date range, category, and branch
- Compliance score per department/branch (0–100)
- Monthly audit summary with downloadable reports
- Escalation queue for transactions pending Shariah board review
- THE AZHAR stablecoin reserve transparency panel — shows asset backing: gold, sovereign fiat, real-world assets via Musharakah contracts
